VIVO 200PRO

Vivo 200PRO - Key Specification

Specification Details
Display 6.78-inch quad-curved display, LTPO panel, adaptive 0.1-120 Hz refresh rate, very high peak brightness; Armor Glass build.
Processor MediaTek Dimensity 9400, fabricated on 3 nm process.
Memory & Storage 16 GB RAM + 512 GB internal storage; LPDDR5X RAM, UFS 4.0 storage.
Camera – Main: 50 MP (SONY LYT-818), f/1.57 aperture, wide-angle, true colour with ZEISS optics.
– Telephoto: 200 MP APO periscope, ~85 mm equivalent, f/2.67; large sensor, good zoom performance.
– Ultra-wide: 50 MP, autofocusing ultra-wide lens.
